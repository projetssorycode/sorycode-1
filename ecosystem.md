A collection of community projects built on SoryCode.

:::note
Want to add your SoryCode related project to this list? Submit a PR.
:::

You can also check out [awesome-sorycode](https://github.com/awesome-opencode/awesome-opencode) and [sorycode.cafe](https://opencode.cafe), a community that aggregates the ecosystem and community.
